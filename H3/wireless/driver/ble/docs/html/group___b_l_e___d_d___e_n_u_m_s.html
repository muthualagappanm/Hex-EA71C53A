enum BLE_DD_EventId_T

The definition of BLE_DD module event ID.

Enumerator
BLE_DD_EVT_DISC_COMPLETE 

The discovery of all service is completed. BLE_DD_EvtDiscResult_T

BLE_DD_EVT_DISC_SUCCESS 

The discovery of specific service is completed and the service is found. BLE_DD_EvtDiscResult_T

BLE_DD_EVT_DISC_SVC_NOT_FOUND 

The discovery of specific service is completed but the service is not found. BLE_DD_EvtDiscResult_T

BLE_DD_EVT_SEC_REQUIRED 

Encryption/authentication permission is required accessing characteristic at remote. Application might need to initiate encryption/authentication to remote. BLE_DD_EvtSecRequire_T

BLE_DD_EVT_CONNECTED 

BLE link is established. See BLE_DD_EvtConnect_T for the event details.

BLE_DD_EVT_DISCONNECTED 

BLE link is terminated. See BLE_DD_EvtDisconnect_T for the event details.
